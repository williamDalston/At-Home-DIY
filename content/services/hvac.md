## Overview

Your HVAC system keeps your home comfortable year-round. From routine maintenance to emergency repairs and full system replacements, our directory connects you with licensed, certified HVAC professionals in your area.

## Common HVAC Services

- **AC repair and maintenance** — Diagnosing and fixing cooling issues, refrigerant recharge, cleaning coils, and seasonal tune-ups.
- **Furnace repair and installation** — Gas and electric furnace diagnostics, ignitor replacement, heat exchanger inspection, and new installations.
- **Heat pump services** — Installation, repair, and maintenance of air-source and ground-source heat pumps.
- **Ductwork** — Duct cleaning, sealing, insulation, and new duct installation or modification.
- **Thermostat services** — Smart thermostat installation, programming, and Wi-Fi setup.
- **Indoor air quality** — Humidifiers, dehumidifiers, air purifiers, and UV germicidal lights.
- **Mini-split systems** — Ductless heating and cooling installation for additions, garages, and specific rooms.

## Choosing an HVAC Contractor

1. **NATE certification** — North American Technician Excellence certification indicates advanced training.
2. **EPA Section 608 certification** — Required for handling refrigerants.
3. **State licensing** — Verify through your state contractor licensing board.
4. **Insurance** — Liability and workers compensation coverage.
5. **Written estimates** — Detailed quotes before work begins with equipment model numbers specified.
