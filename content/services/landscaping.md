## Overview

Professional landscaping enhances your home's curb appeal, increases property value, and creates outdoor living spaces your family can enjoy. From basic lawn maintenance to complete landscape design, our directory connects you with experienced local professionals.

## Common Landscaping Services

- **Lawn care** — Mowing, edging, fertilization, aeration, overseeding, and weed control.
- **Landscape design** — Custom garden designs, planting plans, and 3D visualizations.
- **Planting** — Trees, shrubs, perennials, annuals, and ground cover installation.
- **Hardscaping** — Patios, walkways, retaining walls, outdoor kitchens, and fire pits.
- **Irrigation** — Sprinkler system installation, repair, and winterization.
- **Tree services** — Pruning, removal, stump grinding, and health assessment.
- **Mulching and bed maintenance** — Garden bed preparation, mulch installation, and seasonal cleanup.
- **Outdoor lighting** — Landscape and path lighting design and installation.

## Choosing a Landscaper

1. **Portfolio** — Ask to see photos of completed projects similar to yours.
2. **Insurance** — Liability insurance is essential; workers comp for crews.
3. **Design experience** — For larger projects, choose a landscape designer or architect.
4. **Plant knowledge** — A good landscaper recommends plants suited to your climate zone and soil.
5. **Written contract** — Should detail scope, materials, timeline, and payment schedule.
