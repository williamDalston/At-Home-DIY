## Overview

Whether you need a quick faucet repair or a complete re-pipe, finding a qualified local plumber is essential. Our directory connects you with licensed, insured plumbing professionals who handle everything from routine maintenance to emergency repairs.

## Common Plumbing Services

- **Drain cleaning** — Professional clearing of clogged kitchen, bathroom, and main sewer drains using snakes, hydro-jetting, or camera inspection.
- **Leak detection and repair** — Finding and fixing leaks in supply lines, drain pipes, faucets, and fixtures. Includes slab leak detection.
- **Water heater services** — Installation, repair, and replacement of tank and tankless water heaters.
- **Fixture installation** — Installing new faucets, toilets, sinks, showers, and bathtubs.
- **Pipe repair and replacement** — Repairing burst, corroded, or frozen pipes. Includes re-piping for older homes.
- **Garbage disposal** — Installation and repair of kitchen garbage disposals.
- **Sewer line services** — Camera inspection, cleaning, repair, and replacement of main sewer lines.
- **Gas line services** — Installation and repair of gas lines for water heaters, stoves, and dryers.

## Choosing a Plumber

Look for these qualifications when selecting a plumber:

1. **Valid state license** — Verify through your state licensing board.
2. **Insurance** — General liability and workers compensation.
3. **Experience** — At least 3-5 years of residential plumbing experience.
4. **Reviews** — Check online reviews and ask for references.
5. **Written estimates** — Get detailed quotes before work begins.
6. **Warranty** — Look for a workmanship guarantee of at least 1 year.
